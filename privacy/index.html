Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026

What data is collected

When you connect accounts through Plaid, the app receives bank account details (such as institution and account names, account type, and balances) and transaction data.

How data is stored

Data is stored in application storage with encryption safeguards in transit and at rest. Access is restricted to the account owner.

Data sales and sharing

Data is not sold. Data is not monetized.

Deletion requests

To request deletion of your connected account and transaction data, email the contact address below. Requests are handled by removing stored records tied to your account.
