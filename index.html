Budget Tracker

Personal budget and account overview

This app is a private budget tracker for reviewing account balances, spending activity, and month-to-date progress in one place.

You can connect supported financial institutions through Plaid, including institutions that use OAuth-style authentication such as Chase and Schwab.

Bank and transaction data is encrypted in storage and transit, and your data is never sold.
